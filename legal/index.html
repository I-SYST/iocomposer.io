3) Externally Retrieved SDKs (Not Bundled)

Downloaded SDKs are stored in a separate folder named external/ on the user's machine.

IOcomposer fetches these components unmodified from upstream sources where possible, and the installer may record the upstream repository URL (and optionally tag/commit) used for traceability.

IOcomposer does not bundle third-party vendor SDKs within the product distribution. Where required, IOcomposer may download third-party SDKs and related components directly from their official upstream repositories (for example, GitHub) onto the user's machine.
